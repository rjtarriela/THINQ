Accountancy & Business Management (ABM)

    The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand combines creativity, quantitative application, and business savvy to prepare tomorrow's greatest business professionals. Students that study ABM in senior high school will learn about financial management, business management, corporate operations, and accounting. Students who choose this path will be well-prepared for a variety of occupations as managers, accountants, and business owners. These ABM courses will provide students with the industry knowledge and skills they need to excel in their careers and run their enterprises successfully.

   The ABM strand curriculum was designed specifically to provide students with the communication and socialization skills that will set them apart as world-class professionals in a highly competitive industry, as well as the business savvy, mathematical, and financial know-how required for resource management and day-to-day operations. Finally, ABM graduates will be provided a choice of main disciplines dealing with Accountancy, Statistics, and Business Mathematics in order to gain industry-standard knowledge. Enroll in the ABM strand to sharpen your business acumen and empower yourself with the tools you need to succeed.

The ABM strand prepares senior high school students for success by providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge from the converging subject matter of Accounting, Business, and Management. Enrolling in the ABM Strand provides the following advantages:

  1. Improves senior high school students' confidence in communicating their ideas, allowing them to have more faith in their notions and resourcefulness throughout their ABM course projects.
  2. Instills the values of honesty and excellent business practice that are required for a field that works with finances and commercial relationships, while also adopting a mindset that actively seeks solutions to any crisis that may arise.
  3. Improve their math abilities for rapid and transparent computations, which will allow them to make smart business judgments and fulfill accounting chores with attention to detail and precision later in their professions.
